8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Encryption at rest: Your data is encrypted at rest on Supabase infrastructure (AES-256)
  • Authentication: Secure authentication via Supabase Auth with support for email/password and Google OAuth 2.0
  • Row-Level Security (RLS): Database-level access policies ensure each user can only access their own data
  • Secure credential storage: Sensitive tokens are stored using platform-native secure storage (Keychain on iOS, EncryptedSharedPreferences on Android)
  • Access control: Administrative access to production systems is restricted and audited

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Automated Decision-Making & AI Processing

FinFlect uses automated processing in the following ways:

9.1 Financial Health Score

Automatically calculated based on your income, expenses, emergency fund coverage, insurance status, and debt-to-income ratios. This score is for informational and educational purposes only. It does not result in any legal or similarly significant effects on you, and no actions are taken automatically based on this score.

9.2 Advisor Recommendations

Generated automatically based on your financial profile (e.g., suggesting you build an emergency fund, review insurance, or reduce debt). These are non-binding suggestions only and do not constitute financial advice. You are free to follow or disregard them.

9.3 AI Chat (Google Gemini)

When you use the AI chat feature:

  • Your financial context (summary data, not raw transaction records) and your chat messages are sent to Google Gemini AI to generate responses
  • Google processes this data under their Privacy Policy and API Terms of Service
  • We do not use your data to train or fine-tune any AI models
  • AI responses are informational only and do not constitute professional financial advice

9.4 Your Rights Regarding Automated Processing

Under GDPR Article 22 and India DPDP Act, you have the right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing that produce legal effects or similarly significantly affect you. Our automated features produce informational recommendations only and do not make binding decisions about you. If you have concerns about any automated processing, please contact us.
