Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

FinFlect ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal financial planning app designed to help you manage your money, track expenses, set goals, and improve your financial health. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and profile details (such as monthly income, dependents count, and currency preference) to personalize your experience.

Financial Data: You may enter transactions, budgets, goals, loans, and insurance details. This data is stored securely to provide our services.

Device Information: We may collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for improving compatibility and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the app's features
  • To generate personalized financial recommendations and health scores
  • To display your budgets, goals, and transaction summaries
  • To send notifications related to your financial activity (if enabled)
  • To provide AI-powered financial insights

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (cloud infrastructure). We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest. Authentication is handled securely via Supabase Auth with support for email/password and Google Sign-In.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Backend, database, and authentication
  • Google Sign-In — Optional authentication method
  • RevenueCat — Subscription and in-app purchase management
  • Google Gemini AI — AI-powered financial insights and chat

These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your financial data is only used within the app to provide you with our services. We may share data only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

6. Family Features

If you use family sharing features, certain financial summaries may be shared with family members you explicitly invite. You control what data is shared within your family group.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Update or correct your information via the app settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your financial data

9. Children's Privacy

FinFlect is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above.
